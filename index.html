4l1c3 (b. 1997, tenerife) is a multidisciplinary artist, community builder and event organizer based in berlin.

her practice moves across photography, painting, drawing and collage — often circling around grief, the body, slowness and the interior life.

she also runs the neurodivergent creative club, a weekly event in berlin for neurodivergent people to connect and create together.
